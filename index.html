About Me

Hi everyone, I'm Rachel! I'm a freshman at University of Washington-Seattle studying Computer Science. I'm a highly innovative, passionate, and motivated individual, and I'm excited to dive more into web development!

More About Me

I'm from Issaquah, Washington, but was born in Kirkland, WA. I love the outdoors, whether its hiking, boating, or biking! Nothing beats the beauty of the PNW. In my spare time, I love hanging out with friends or partaking in some of my hobbies from baking to photography!

I'm passionate about utilizing technology to improve my community. I possess excellent analytical and interpersonal skills with a demonstrated commitment to excellence. I am dedicated to leveraging my strong organizational abilities and leadership skills to collaborate with others.

Currently, I'm a part of UW's Society of Women Engineers club on the Community Outreach Committee. I hope to join UW's WebImpact to not only expand my website development skills, but also form a community of passionate and engaging people!
